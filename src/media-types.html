Media Types Registrations

MathML makes use of three media types: "application/mathml+xml", "application/mathml-presentation+xml" and "application/mathml-content+xml", in conformance with [[RFC4288]] and W3CRegMedia. The media-types have been approved by IESG for registration with IANA as announced by IETF and are visible on the list of application media-types.

The normative text of the registration referenced by IANA is MathML 3 Appendix B Media Types Registrations. These media types apply to all versions of MathML, including MathML 4 and MathML Core. To avoid conflicting definitions, the text of the registrations are not repeated here.

The MathML media types should be used as follows:

"application/mathml-presentation+xml"

MathML instances that consist solely of presentation markup.

"application/mathml-content+xml"

MathML instances that consist solely of content markup.

"application/mathml+xml"

Any valid MathML instance. Must be used for MathML instances that are a mix of presentation and content markup, or where the composition of an instance is not known or cannot be guaranteed.
